Blackpink Arena Tour 2018 'Special Final in Kyocera Dome Osaka (2019)

Kill This Love is the second Korean mini-album by BLACKPINK. It was released on April 5, 2019 with "Kill This Love" serving as the album's title track and "Don't Know What To Do" as a sub-title track.

Background and Release

The Blackpink Arena Tour group's first Japanese concert began in July 2018, visited three cities in Japan and hosted a total of eight concerts, bringing together a total of 125,000 spectators.[2] This video album contains live videos of the final show at the Osaka Dome on December 24, 2018 with the participation of 50,000 fans.

The album's release was originally scheduled for March 13, 2019, but was postponed to March 22.
