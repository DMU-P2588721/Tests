BLACKPINK 2018 TOUR 'IN YOUR AREA' SEOUL (Live)

BLACKPINK 2018 Tour 'In Your Area' Seoul" is the second live album released by BLACKPINK. The album consists of footage from BLACKPINK's first ever solo concert held in Seoul on November 10 and 11, 2018 at Olympic Gymnastics Arena. It was released on August 30, 2019.
