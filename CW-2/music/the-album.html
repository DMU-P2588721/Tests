The Album

The Album (stylized in all uppercase and alternatively titled BLΛƆKPIИK The Album) is the first Korean studio album and second album overall by BLACKPINK. It was released on October, 2, 2020, with "Lovesick Girls" serving as the title track. "How You Like That" and "Ice Cream" were released as pre-released singles.

Background and Release

On May 18, 2020, YG Entertainment revealedBLACKPINK would be doing a three part comeback. It will begin in June with the pre-release single "How You Like That" and end in September. YG revealed that BLACKPINK had recorded more than 10 new songs for their upcoming album. Their first song was to be released in Mid-June as a pre-release single. The second song was a new kind of song scheduled for a release in late July or August. Finally their full length album was revealed to be released in September with no exact date.

On July 23, it was revealed BLACKPINK was gearing up for another single in their 3 step comeback after their successful "How You Like That" release. YG dropped a colorful teaser with the girls above and an empty space with the word "Featuring". It is the 2nd part in the 3 step comeback. Reporters also suspected they would collaborate with Selena Gomez, but no official statement had been announced. Later, the song was confirmed to feature American solo artist Selena Gomez. On August 21, it was revealed that they will be collaborating on the track "Ice Cream."

On July 28, YG dropped a teaser image and the album cover for their long awaited studio album, titled BLACKPINK The Album or THE ALBUM set for release on October 2, 2020. It will be the final part for their 3-part comeback after "How You Like That" and "Ice Cream"

On September 27, it was announced that BLACKPINK will be holding a comeback live event on October 1. On September 28, it was announced that Lovesick Girls will be the title track off their album, The Album. Later that day they also released the album's track list, which revealed that both Jennie and Jisoo participated in writing the lyrics for Lovesick Girls.

Blackpink explained that "The Album" shows a more mature part of the group, through singing not only about love, but diverse emotions experienced by girls growing up". The group also stated that "there are a lot of different emotions, not just love" on the album.

Promotion

BLACKPINK began music show promotions on October 10th at Music Core. For the first week of promotions , they performed both Lovesick Girls and Pretty Savage.

On October 21, BLACKPINK will be live on Tik Tok. The members will be talking about their first full length album, The Album, how they've been doing, playing quizzes, and will be interacting with fans through various corners.
