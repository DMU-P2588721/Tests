BLACKPINK 2019-2020 World Tour In Your Area - Tokyo Dome

BLACKPINK 2019-2020 World Tour In Your Area - Tokyo Dome is the second Japanese, and third live album by BLACKPINK. It was released on May 14, 2020 and consists of footage from their Tokyo Dome concert on December 4, 2019, during the BLACKPINK 2019-2020 World Tour [In Your Area].
