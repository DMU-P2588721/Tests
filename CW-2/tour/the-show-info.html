BLACKPINK: The Show

BLACKPINK: The Show is a live streamed concert that was originally supposed to held on December 27, 2020 until it was postponed due to COVID-19 restrictions. It is was held on 31st January, 2021. The concert was live streamed from their Youtube Channel at 2 PM KST. It was exclusive to members and sponsored by Tokopedia and Globe.

Background

On January 25, 2021, it was reported that Ros√© will be performing her new song from her solo album for the first time at the concert. Before her performance, half of the music video for her solo debut was shown. She then went on to perform her b-side track, "Gone". The name was officially revealed after the performance.

On January 26, behind the scenes content was posted on BLACKPINK's YouTube channel. Only members can gain access to these exclusive videos.

A sneak peak of the livestream concert was aired on The Late Late Show with James Corden on January 28. The sneak peak was the first part of the concert stage where they performed Pretty Savage with a band. A YG official stated that the performance that was aired on The James Corden Show was pre-recorded and is not the same as concert stage.

YG Entertainment announced that the concert will have three main sets that will be transformed into 10 different stages, also including a never before seen special stage.

On January 30, the soundcheck rehearsal was aired live at 2pm KST on BLACKPINK's YouTube Channel. This was exclusive to plus members.

The concert was rebroadcasted on February 7 and February 14 at 3:00am, 11:00am, 4:00pm, and 7:00pm KST.
