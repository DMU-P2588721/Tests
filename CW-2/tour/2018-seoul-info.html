BLACKPINK 2018 Tour [In Your Area] Seoul x BC Card is Black Pink's first solo concert in Seoul. It was held on November 10 and 11, 2018 at Olympic Gymnastics Arena with 15,000 capacity. YG Entertainment announced the news through BLACKPINK's Official Korean BLINK Zone.

The concert tickets for day one were sold out 2-3 minutes through an Auction website on September 18, 2018.

Background

On 12th September 2018, it was announced that the group would hold their first ever concert in Seoul titled "Black Pink 2018 Tour [In Your Area] Seoul x BC Card" at Olympic Gymnastics Arena.
